it just shows me how Mainland Chinese people as a whole are suffering from a form of PTSD from the Opium Wars and the Eight Nation Alliance. I think this is the main problem with you. Yes, we understand fully and deeply what these activists feel, about when sympathy for a stranger leads to a strong will to fulfill justice and then to contribute to a change of the society, of the system, and of the world. What you have been questioning about is the basic stuff of humanity, and yes, we commies are humans, not red orcs.

The way that we chose to act however is different, which originates from cultural reasons, and by culture I mean the convolution of everything that has happened to the community that we consider as Chinese in history.
